About Stratagem

A student-led think tank at Jadavpur University, dedicated to structured thinking, strategic dialogue, and interdisciplinary inquiry.

What is Stratagem?

Stratagem is Jadavpur University’s first student-led think tank — a platform built to encourage structured thinking, strategic dialogue, and interdisciplinary problem-solving among students.

Why Stratagem Exists

Universities generate knowledge, but often lack spaces where students can apply it across disciplines. Stratagem exists to bridge that gap — by fostering critical inquiry, informed debate, and research-driven perspectives on contemporary issues.

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of students who think beyond textbooks, question assumptions, and contribute meaningfully to society through strategic thought and intellectual rigor.

Our Mission

  • Promote interdisciplinary discussions on policy, technology, economics, and society
  • Encourage student-led research and publications
  • Create forums for dialogue with academics, professionals, and policymakers
  • Develop analytical and strategic thinking skills among members

Core Values

• Intellectual honesty

• Curiosity and open inquiry

• Evidence-based reasoning

• Collaboration across disciplines
